Niveau 1

Mira a los animales.

Se på dyrene.

Look at the animals.

La vaca dice, “Muu.”

Koen siger "muh".

The cow says, "Moo."

La cabra dice, “Be, be.”

Geden siger "bæh, bæh".

The goat says, "Meh, meh."

El caballo dice, “Hin.”

Hesten siger "pruh".

The horse says, "Neigh."

El cerdo dice, “Oink-oink.”

Grisen siger "øf".

The pig says, "Grunt."

La gallina dice, “Cló-cló.”

Hønen siger "gok".

The chicken says, "Cluck."

El perro dice, “Guau.”

Hunden siger "vov".

The dog says, "Woof."

El granjero dice, “¡Chsss!”

Landmanden siger "shh!"

The farmer says, "Shhh!"
